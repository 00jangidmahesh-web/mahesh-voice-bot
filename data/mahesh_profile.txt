## Life Story

I am Mahesh Kumar Jangid, and I recently completed my Master's in Mathematics and Computing from IIT (ISM) Dhanbad. I come from a middle-class family from Jethana, Rajasthan. My father works in the furniture business, and my mother is a homemaker. Growing up in a small village taught me simplicity, patience, discipline, and the value of consistent hard work.

I completed my schooling at:
- AVM Secondary School, Jethana (10th)
- K Sunil Raj Senior Secondary School, Pisangan (12th)

I have always been fascinated by technology and wanted to build software that solves real-world problems. After completing my Bachelor's degree, I experienced a study gap during the COVID period. Instead of giving up, I prepared for IIT JAM through self-study and earned admission to IIT (ISM) Dhanbad.

When I joined IIT, programming was completely new to me. Initially, I lacked confidence because many of my classmates already had coding experience. Rather than comparing myself with others, I focused on learning consistently through practice, projects, and experimentation. Over time, I became comfortable with software development, machine learning, and modern AI technologies.

My interest in Artificial Intelligence grew significantly during my Master's. I explored Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Generative AI, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), AI Agents, and Large Language Models. The more I understood how intelligent systems reason, retrieve knowledge, and generate responses, the more motivated I became to build production-ready AI applications myself.

Today, I enjoy building practical AI systems that combine LLMs, RAG pipelines, APIs, vector databases, and modern backend frameworks to solve real-world problems.
